# Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Window

Refunds are available:
- within 14 days of purchase

After 14 days:
- refunds are not guaranteed
- account credits may be offered instead

---

## Enterprise Contracts

Enterprise contracts:
- generally non-refundable
- may qualify for SLA credits

---

## Churn Retention Playbook

For high-value customers threatening churn:
- escalate to retention team
- offer onboarding assistance
- offer account credits
- prioritize executive review

Public review threats must be escalated immediately.

---

## AI Chatbot Incorrect Guidance

If internal AI systems provided incorrect policy guidance:
- acknowledge customer frustration
- avoid admitting legal liability
- escalate internally for review

---

## Refund Exceptions

Exceptions may be approved for:
- duplicate charges
- platform outages
- severe billing errors

Requires finance approval.
