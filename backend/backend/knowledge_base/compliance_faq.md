# Compliance FAQ

## HIPAA

Enterprise customers may request:
- HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

Requires:
- Enterprise plan
- Security review

---

## GDPR

Customers may request:
- data portability
- deletion
- export requests

Legal response window:
- 30 days

GDPR requests must be escalated immediately.

---

## SOC 2

Platform status:
- SOC 2 Type II compliant

Audit reports available upon request.

---

## Data Residency

Supported regions:
- US
- EU
- APAC

Enterprise-only feature.

---

## Security Incidents

Security threats including:
- ransomware
- suspicious login alerts
- data breach claims

must be escalated to security operations immediately.
