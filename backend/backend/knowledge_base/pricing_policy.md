# Pricing Policy

## Subscription Tiers

### Starter
- $49/month
- Up to 5 users
- Email support only
- API limit: 10,000 requests/month

### Standard
- $199/month
- Up to 50 users
- Priority support
- API limit: 250,000 requests/month

### Enterprise
- Custom pricing
- Unlimited users
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA-backed uptime
- Custom integrations
- Advanced compliance support

---

## Non-Profit Discounts

Verified non-profit organizations receive:
- 30% discount on Standard plan
- Annual billing only
- Requires verification documents

---

## Pro-Rata Billing

When customers upgrade mid-cycle:
- Remaining balance credited automatically
- Charges prorated by remaining billing period

Downgrades:
- Applied next billing cycle
- No retroactive refunds

---

## Enterprise Negotiation Rules

Enterprise customers may receive:
- Multi-year contract discounts
- Custom SLA agreements
- Dedicated onboarding
- Security review support

All enterprise pricing negotiations require sales approval.
