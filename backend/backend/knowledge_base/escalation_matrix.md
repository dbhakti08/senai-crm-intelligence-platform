# Escalation Matrix

## Legal Threats

Examples:
- cease and desist
- lawsuits
- SLA breach legal escalation

Route to:
- Legal Team
- Executive Support

Priority:
- Critical

---

## Security Incidents

Examples:
- ransomware
- suspicious login
- data leak claims

Route to:
- Security Operations

Priority:
- Critical

Never auto-reply to attackers.

---

## VIP Churn Risk

Examples:
- public review threats
- repeated complaints
- executive dissatisfaction

Route to:
- Retention Team
- Customer Success Director

---

## GDPR Requests

Examples:
- Article 20 export
- deletion requests

Route to:
- Compliance Team
- Legal Team

---

## Production Outages

Examples:
- P0 downtime
- silent data corruption
- failed deployments

Route to:
- Incident Response Team
- Engineering Leadership
