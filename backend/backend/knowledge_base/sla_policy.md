# SLA Policy

## Uptime Commitment

Enterprise customers receive:
- 99.9% monthly uptime SLA

Standard customers:
- Best effort support
- No financial SLA credits

---

## Incident Severity Levels

### P0 Critical
Examples:
- Production outage
- Data corruption
- Security breach

Response Time:
- 15 minutes

RCA Delivery:
- Within 24 hours

---

### P1 High
Examples:
- Major degraded functionality
- Login issues

Response Time:
- 1 hour

---

### P2 Medium
Examples:
- Minor feature degradation
- Reporting delays

Response Time:
- 4 hours

---

## SLA Credits

If uptime falls below SLA:

| Uptime | Credit |
|--------|--------|
| 99.0%–99.8% | 10% |
| 95.0%–98.9% | 25% |
| Below 95% | 50% |

Credits apply to next billing cycle only.

---

## Legal Escalations

Any legal escalation involving SLA disputes must:
- be routed to Legal Team
- include full thread history
- include incident RCA
